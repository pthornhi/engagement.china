History of China Office:
The University of Illinois inaugurated its Shanghai Office in December of 2013 and two full-time employees, both Chinese nationals, were hired in April 2014. The Shanghai Office resulted from the University's partnership with the State of Illinois Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity (DCEO). DCEO opened an office in China more than twenty years ago and it operates through the US Department of Commerce office in Shanghai. The Illinois office in Shanghai is part of the DCEO office, which enables us to leverage State of Illinois partnerships throughout China.
