Current Students and Parents

Are you a student looking for an internship or full-time position in China? There are a number of US-based and multinational firms that have locations in China, and of course there are thousands of Chinese companies. Many companies that are recruiting for positions in China will post them on the I-Link system (link to https://i-link-illinois-csm.symplicity.com/) at the University. Networking is very important if you want to get a job in China. We have thousands of alumni who live and work in China and they are very interested in connecting with current students! There are several ways to develop a network among our Chinese alumni:

  • Attend a career day in a major Chinese city (Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou/Shenzhen)
  • Participate in the Chinese alumni mentoring program
  • Join one or more U of I alumni WeChat groups

If you are a parent of a current student and need to get in touch with your student but have been unable to, please contact the Emergency Dean.
