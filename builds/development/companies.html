Companies

A land grant institution, the University of Illinois has a long record of commitment to public The University of Illinois partners with companies that operate in China in a number of ways.

Job and internship opportunities
Are you interested in hiring the best talent? Hire Illinois students and graduates! We encourage Chinese companies and companies that operate in China to consider Illinois students for internship or full-time job opportunities. You can post internship and job postings for no cost on the UIUC recruiting system. Visit https://i-link-illinois-csm.symplicity.com/ to register at no cost and for more information. You can post jobs at any time for students from across the Illinois campus. Learn more about Illinois rankings and programs at http://illinois.edu/about/overview/facts/facts.html and http://illinois.edu/about/overview/facts/rankings.html.

Executive education programs
Illinois offers a variety of executive education programs, ranging from higher education to business to other areas. We would be happy to help you find the program that is right for you and your staff. Contact the Shanghai Office to learn more about what program may be right for you.

Research
Illinois is a recognized throughout the world for its leadership in research and scholarship. We collaborate with a number of companies and universities within and outside the United States on research projects and common interests. Please contact the Shanghai Office if you are interested in working with us on research activities. The staff in Shanghai can help you to identify what area(s) of the institution may be the best fit for your interests and strengths.
