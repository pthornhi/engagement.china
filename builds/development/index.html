The University of Illinois enjoys a long history with China, beginning in the early twentieth century. In fact, Illinois was influential in the establishment and development of Tsinghua University in China. Currently, the University community includes more than 5,000 Chinese students, making Illinois the top destination for Chinese students in the world, as well as one of the top three institutions in sending students to study in China.
