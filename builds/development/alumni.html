Alumni

Alumni have formed a number of alumni groups in several major cities throughout China, including Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Hong Kong. If you are interested in joining an alumni group, please contact the Shanghai Office to find out where there may be a group located near you.

The alumni groups each plan their own activities. Events range from talks and presentations from faculty and business leaders, social gatherings after work, more formal events or banquets, etc. Most of the alumni groups that have formed gather at least once each month. These gatherings are a great way to keep in touch with alumni and meet new people.

If you are looking for a job, alumni groups can provide a strong network of support and connections. Illinois alumni in China work in a variety of positions, industries, and fields. We have alumni in large companies, technical roles, foreign service, consulting, etc.

Current students appreciate the opportunity to connect with alumni as well. We are working with alumni in China to develop career and professional development programs for current students and recent graduates, including career days and job fairs in China and a student-alumni mentoring program.

Alumni can also assist by encouraging high school students to include the University of Illinois in their list of colleges that they consider, and to apply. Sharing your experience is a great way to encourage others to become a fellow Illini.

Learn more about alumni groups and activities by joining one or more WeChat groups for UIUC alumni in China. (link to groups?)
