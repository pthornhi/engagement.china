Services and Activities

The Shanghai Office provides a number of services to the campus:
  • Establish and develop relationships with companies in China to identify opportunities for internship and full-time positions for students and alumni, executive education, and joint research projects
  • Assist in promoting University activities and programs in China
  • Support alumni networks and activities in China by assisting with logistics and other resources
  • Maintain relationships with Chinese academic institutions
  • Support incoming student orientation planning
  • Assist University of Illinois faculty/staff visiting China
